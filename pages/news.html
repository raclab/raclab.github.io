2024

RACLAB published "Consensus-based Virtual Leader Tracking Swarm Algorithm with GDRRT*-PSO for Path-Planning of Multiple UAVs" in Swarm and Evolutionary Computation, a highly regarded SCI-E indexed journal.
📄 Publication 2024 · Swarm and Evolutionary Computation · SCI-E

The 25th National Conference on Automatic Control (TOK 2024) was held in Konya. RACLAB contributed 8 research papers covering autonomous driving, quadrotor control, UAV tracking, and traffic signal systems — a record for a single conference.

2022

🏆 RACLAB SİGUN — TEKNOFEST 2022 CHAMPION!
RACLAB SİGUN won first place in the TEKNOFEST 2022 Robotaxi Passenger Autonomous Vehicle competition! The team's deep learning-based perception and autonomous navigation system outperformed all competitors. This was RACLAB's first national robotaxi championship.
🏆 Champion 2022 · TEKNOFEST · Robotaxi Competition Team Page →
📄 IEEE Access — Systematic Literature Review on EKF-Based SLAM (2022)
RACLAB published a comprehensive systematic literature review on Extended Kalman Filter-based SLAM for autonomous navigation in IEEE Access, becoming a key reference in the robotics field.
📄 Publication 2022 · IEEE Access · SCI-E
📄 4 SCI-E Publications in 2022 — Deep Learning & Robotics
RACLAB had a prolific year with 4 SCI-E publications covering CNNs vs Capsule Networks, CNN for pump status classification, medical image segmentation (2D/3D), and EKF-SLAM — across Applied Sciences, Engineering Science and Technology, IEEE Access, and Mathematical Biosciences and Engineering.
📄 Publications 2022 · Multiple SCI-E Journals View All →

📄 Publication 2021 · Electronics Letters · SCI-E

🎉 RACLAB Founded at Konya Technical University (2014)
The Robotics, Automation and Control Laboratory (RACLAB) was established at what was then Selçuk University (now Konya Technical University) by Prof. Akif DURDU. RACLAB started with autonomous UAV research and TÜBİTAK 2209-A student projects, quickly growing into a comprehensive robotics research center.
📢 Milestone 2014 · KTUN · Foundation
📄 Elektronika ir Elektrotechnika — Modified PMSM Control (2017)
RACLAB's early publication "Modified Control of PMSM using Backstepping Design" appeared in Elektronika ir Elektrotechnika (SCI-E indexed), demonstrating the lab's foundational work in control systems before expanding into robotics.
📄 Publication 2017 · Elektronika ir Elektrotechnika · SCI-E
