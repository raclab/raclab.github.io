News from RACLAB

Competition results, research milestones, awards, and announcements from our teams at Konya Technical University.

2025

📅 26 September 2025

Our members Halil İbrahim Köse and Harun Günay received an Honorable Mention – Most Creative Award at the "Accessible and Connected Türkiye 2053" R&D Competition of Ministry of Transport and Infrastructure with their project "An Autonomous AI-based Guardian Protecting the Blue Homeland." We warmly congratulate our teammates for making us proud and wish them continued success in many more innovative projects.🎉 (link)

📅 17–21 September 2025

At the TEKNOFEST 2025 Digital Technologies in Industry Competition, our team RACLAB FAAL (Mahmut Sami Güvenç (Team Captain), Mirza Şahan Özdemir, Elif Su İbiş, Seher Tiryaki, Meryem Derin, Furkan Yeşildağ, Enes İşcan, and Ayşegül Börekçi) achieved Third Place in Türkiye in the Advanced Category at the competitions organized in collaboration with the Ministry of Industry and Technology! 💪⚙️🎉 (link)

📅 17–21 September 2025

At the TEKNOFEST 2025 International UAV Competition, our team RACLAB TULPARS (Sudenaz Erdoğan (Team Captain), Asude Nur Demir, Eren Berk Saç, Hüseyin Yiğit Bağcı, and Yusuf Sifil) achieved "Third Place" in Türkiye in the Free Category and also received the "Advisor Third Place" award in the competitions organized in collaboration with TÜBİTAK! 💪🚁🎉 (link)

📅 17–21 September 2025

At TEKNOFEST 2025 Robotaxi Passenger Autonomous Vehicle Competition, our team RACLAB SİGUN (Burak Özsoy (Team Captain), Bahtiyar Erener Kurt, Hasan Altuğ, Osman Melih Kocaoğlu, Recep Mahmut Gündüz, Şevval Eymen Nergiz, Fatmanur Çınar, and Enes Kurt) achieved "Second Place" in Türkiye and was also awarded the "Most Original Software" prize at the competitions held in Bilişim Vadisi! 💪🚗🎉 (link)

📅 17–21 September 2025

At TEKNOFEST 2025 Unmanned Underwater Systems Competition, our team RACLAB NOVA (Hatice Kübra İLHAN (Team Captain), Abdurrahman İnce, Beyza Nur YAĞCI, Feyza İnal, Furkan ERANIL, Gizem Ülker, Mevlüt Burak Dağlı, Mustafa Bedir Sarıtosun, Feyza Baltürk, and Ahmet Selim İlhan) reached the top of the podium! 🏆 On top of that, we were also honored with the "Best Team Spirit" award! 💪🎉 (link)

📅 30 August 2025

At the TEKNOFEST 2025 Unmanned Surface Vehicle Competition, our team RACLAB EZEL — Altuğ Köker (Team Captain), Ahmet Berk Çetiner, Esra Küçük, Eyüp Balcı, Halil İbrahim Köse, Harun Günay, Nursima Uzun, Tolga Işık, and Zehra Akış — placed seventh in the performance ranking at the events held in TEKNOFEST Mavi Vatan, Istanbul. 🎉 (link)

📅 28 April 2025

At TEKNO-SEL 2025 (Selçuk University Technology Festival), our students Şahan Mirza Özdemir, Elif Su İbiş, and Seher Tiryaki won Second Place in the Free Project Category! 🎉 (link)

📅 16 April 2025

Our members Halil İbrahim Köse and Eren Berk Saç received an Honorable Mention at the "Accessible and Connected Türkiye 2053" R&D Competition (Ministry of Transport and Infrastructure) with their project "Hava Ray Devriyesi" (Air Rail Patrol). Congratulations! 🎉

2024

📅 November 2024

New paper accepted: "A Unified Monocular Vision-Based Driving Model for Autonomous Vehicles with Multi-Task Capabilities" by Salim Azak, Fırat Bozkaya, Şükrücan Tığlıoğlu, Abdullah Yusefi, Akif DURDU. Published in IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Vehicles (SCI-E, WOS Q1, Scopus Q1), Vol. 10, Issue 9, Sep. 2025.

📅 2024

New paper published: "Consensus-based Virtual Leader Tracking Swarm Algorithm with GDRRT*-PSO for Path-Planning of Multiple UAVs" by Berat YILDIZ, Muhammet Fatih Aslan, Akif DURDU, Ahmet Kayabaşı. Published in Swarm and Evolutionary Computation, Vol. 88, Article 101612 (SCI-E).

2023

  • RACLAB ARES — "Most Original Design" Award in Agricultural UAV Category
  • RACLAB İHA — Performance Award in Rotary-Wing UAV Category
  • RACLAB MERKÜT — Performance Award in Fixed-Wing UAV Category
  • RACLAB SİGUN — Third Place in Robotaxi Performance Ranking

2022

📅 September 2022

At TEKNOFEST 2022, RACLAB teams achieved multiple awards:

  • RACLAB SİGUN — "Most Original Software" Award + 1st in Performance Ranking in Robotaxi Category
  • RACLAB Swarm İHA — "Best Team Spirit" Award in Swarm UAV Category
  • RACLAB — 4th Place in Smart Transportation Category
📅 2022

At TÜBİTAK 2242 University Students Research Project Competition, our students won Second Place in Defense/Space/Aviation category with the project "Autonomous Underwater Vehicle Control Card".

2021
